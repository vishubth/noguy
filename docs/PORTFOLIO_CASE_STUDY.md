# Portfolio Case Study

## Problem
Digital asset fundraising operations often rely on manual verification, spreadsheet tracking and fragmented operational workflows.

## Solution
A centralized platform for investor operations, transaction verification, allocation management and fundraising reporting.

- FastAPI

- Jinja2
